Pumpkin Chipotle Pasta

Description

This simple recipe is a favourite in our house. The smokey chipotle flavour works very well with the smoothness of the sauce. Originally taken from budgetbytes.com, I have made a few modifications to the recipes for my family's dietary needs, but the essense of the recipe remains the same.

Requiring minimal prep, this is a fantastic weeknight meal for family or for company!

Ingredients

  • Penne - 1 Pack
  • Onion - 1 whole, diced
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Olive Oil - 2 Tbsp
  • Chicken Broth - 1 1/2 Cups
  • Pureed Pumpkin - 1 Can
  • Chipotle Powder - 1 Tsp
  • Coconut Milk - 1 Can, cream only
  • Sausages - 4 cooked links sliced
  • Parmesan Cheese - Optional, to top

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil, cook pasta as per package.
  2. Heat a saute pan over medium heat. Add the oil and onions, cooking until translucent.
  3. Add the sausages and fry until browned. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute more.
  4. Add the chipotle powder, pumpkin, and chicken broth. Cook until heated through, stirring throughout.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the pan, stir to combine. Add the coconut milk cream, and salt to taste.
  6. Serve topped with parmesan cheese.
